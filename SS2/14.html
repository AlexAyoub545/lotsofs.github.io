Chapter 9 - Spiders

  • A spider spawns in front of the exit gate.
  • 5 seconds after killing the first spider spawns 3 more spiders left and right of the exit, and in front of the canyon you came from.
  • 5 more seconds later, another spider spawns in front of the exit gate. Killing it starts an 18 second respawn timer.
  • Yet another 5 seconds later, a spider spawns to the right of the entry canyon (when looking at it). Killing it starts a 13 second timer for a spider left of the canyon/to the side of the battlefield.
  • All spiders must be killed to unlock the trigger that ends the map.
