Fixed/Slave Spawns:

The injured mechanic by the red van always spawns. An additional civilian spawns if a suspect upstairs does.

Map:

A circle denotes a potential spawnpoint (O)
Patrol spawns are marked with a line coming out the bottom left of the circle. (P)
Some spawns are investigator spawns, meaning they will walk towards you if you make any noise. These spawns have a line coming out the bottom right of the circle. (Q)
A line coming from both sides means both patrol and investigators (R)
Slave indicators: Circled = slave. Gradient = antislave.
A cross denotes incapacitated spawns (X).
Superscript 1 = only used when entering from primary. Superscript 2 = same, but for secondary.
