Slave Spawns:

If Alex Jimenez spawns in the dining area:

  • An extra patron will spawn in front of him.
  • An extra patrolling civvy waiter will spawn at the counter 50% of the time. It is unclear what happens the other 50% of the time.
  • An extra bodyguard goon will spawn upstairs at 80% chance.
  • Lian Niu will NOT spawn behind the counter or by the bathrooms.

If Lian Niu spawns in the back alley:

  • A civvy chef will spawn beside him.

Map:

Patrol spawns are marked with a P. These spawnees will immediately follow a predetermined path.
Dots marked with a small I (India) are investigator spawns. These goons will walk towards you if you make any kind of noise.
Slave indicators: Solid lines = slave. Dotted line = antislave.
